How multi-car bundling works

Instead of paying for each car separately, all vehicles in a household go on one policy. Most US insurers offer a multi-vehicle discount on top of the base rate — typically 10-25% lower per car than individual policies.

The bigger benefit: if you also own a home, you can usually bundle home + auto for an additional 10-20% off.
