See This Before You Renew Your Auto Insurance In 2026

Renewal is the easiest moment to switch — and the one most drivers skip.

When your auto renewal letter arrives, the easiest thing to do is nothing. The new premium is set, the date is fixed, and another year just rolls forward.

But renewal is actually the most efficient moment to compare and switch. You have a fresh start date, a known number to beat, and a few minutes of buffer.

3 things US drivers check before renewing

1 What comparable coverage costs elsewhere. Get 3-4 quotes for the exact same liability and collision limits. Top US providers offer rates starting around $30/mo for qualifying drivers.
2 Whether your driving habits qualify for a lower tier. If you drive under 50 miles a day, you may qualify for low-mileage or pay-per-mile pricing — separate from the standard quote.
3 Whether bundling home and auto changes the math. Homeowners typically save 10-20% additional with a combined policy.
The comparison is free and takes about 30 seconds. Nothing changes on your current policy until you actively choose to switch.
