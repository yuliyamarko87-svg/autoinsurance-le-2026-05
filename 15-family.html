What changes for family policies in 2026

Three things to know if you're insuring a household this year:

1. Multi-car is almost always cheaper than separate policies. Even if you and your partner have different driving records, combining vehicles on one policy typically saves 15-25% per car.
2. Homeowner bundles compound. Family + home bundle stacks discounts (10-25% multi-car + 10-20% bundle).
3. Teen drivers don't have to break the budget. Many carriers offer good-student and driver-monitoring discounts that can offset teen rates significantly.
